Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Helios.

What's the difference between Helios Books and Command Center? ▼

Helios Books are comprehensive deep research reports: the foundation. Command Center is the platform where you analyze, store, and transform that research into campaign-ready content. You can use them together or separately.

Can I bring my own research to the Command Center? ▼

Yes. If you already have research from another firm, you can upload it to the Command Center and use all our analysis and content generation tools.

How fast can I get a Helios Book? ▼

What takes traditional firms weeks, we deliver in days. Rush delivery options are also available for time-sensitive situations.

What platforms do you analyze for social media? ▼

We cover Twitter/X, Threads, BlueSky, Reddit, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube. Our AI detects controversies, contradictions, and patterns across all platforms.

Can I research multiple candidates at once? ▼

Yes. Unlike traditional firms that work one target at a time, Helios enables parallel research across multiple candidates. Perfect for PACs and consultants.

What is Concierge? ▼

Concierge is our white-glove service for campaigns that need a dedicated intelligence team, not just software. You get unlimited research requests, dedicated analysts, and priority turnaround.

Who can benefit from these tools? ▼

Congressional campaigns, PACs, Super PACs, nonprofits, and political consultants. We work with organizations of all sizes.
