Civly Launched AI-Powered Campaign Intelligence Platform, Transforming Research for Political Campaigns

North Carolina-based startup delivers comprehensive candidate research in days instead of weeks

Durham, NC. — Civly, a North Carolina-based technology company, today announced the launch of its AI-powered campaign intelligence platform designed to revolutionize how political campaigns, PACs, advocacy organizations, and public affairs firms conduct opposition research.

A core product, branded as Helios, addresses a longstanding pain point in political campaigns: the time-intensive process of compiling comprehensive research on opponents. What traditionally takes research firms weeks to deliver, Civly's technology accomplishes in days.

"Political campaigns operate on tight timelines, and the research that informs strategy shouldn't be a bottleneck," said Dan Barkhuff, CEO of Civly. "We built Helios to give campaigns a decisive edge, actionable intelligence delivered at the speed modern campaigns demand."

A Complete Intelligence Suite

Civly offers several core products designed to work together as a comprehensive campaign intelligence system:

Helios: Deep research books that serve as the foundation for campaign strategy. Aggregates and analyzes data from social media history, voting records, public statements, FEC filings, news coverage, and documented contradictions. Helios delivers in days with on-demand refresh capability as news breaks.

Command Center: Tracks social media, issues, voting records, FEC data, and media coverage continuously, alerting campaigns to relevant developments as they happen so rapid response teams can act before narratives take hold. Our Command Center helps keep all your research organized with the vault.

Compliance: Supports campaign compliance needs, helping teams navigate the complex regulatory landscape of political campaigns. From FEC filing requirements to disclosure obligations, Civly ensures campaigns stay on the right side of the rules while focusing on winning.

Built by Campaign Professionals

Civly was founded by a team combining over 100 races of campaign experience and multi-million dollar PAC leadership. This isn't technology built in a vacuum. It's built by people who have lived the campaign cycle and understand the pressure of rapid response and debate prep.
