Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Helios Core and Helios Complete? ▼

Helios Core provides focused opposition research with key vulnerabilities, social media analysis, and voting records. Helios Complete (coming soon) is a comprehensive deep-dive with exhaustive historical analysis, detailed source documentation, and strategic recommendations.

How long does delivery take? ▼

Helios Core is delivered within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery options are available for time-sensitive situations.

What information do you need from me? ▼

After purchase, we'll ask for basic information about your research target: their name, position/office, and any specific areas of concern you'd like us to focus on.

Can I get research on multiple candidates? ▼

Yes! Each Helios Core covers one target. For multiple candidates, you can purchase additional books. Contact us for volume pricing if you need research on 3+ candidates.

Is there a refund policy? ▼

Due to the nature of custom research, we don't offer refunds once work has begun. However, if you're not satisfied with the research quality, contact us and we'll work to make it right.
