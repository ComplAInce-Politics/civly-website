Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Civly Compliance.

What compliance databases do you screen against? ▼

We screen against 40+ sanctions lists including OFAC, UN sanctions, EU sanctions, the LDA lobbyist database, and various watchlists for prohibited sources.

Does this work with my existing campaign finance software? ▼

Yes. We integrate with major campaign finance platforms and can import data from most common formats including CSV, FEC files, and direct API connections.

How does the FEC filing feature work? ▼

We automatically generate properly formatted .fec files ready for submission. The system handles all the complex formatting requirements for Form 3, 3X, and 3P filings.

What if we get audited? ▼

Every verification decision and compliance action is documented with timestamps and reasoning. You can export complete audit trails at any time.

Is this separate from the Helios research platform? ▼

Yes. Civly Compliance is a separate product focused entirely on donor verification and FEC compliance. You can use it independently or alongside Helios.
