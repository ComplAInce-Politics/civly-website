Meet the Team

Daniel Barkhuff

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

A 2001 US Naval Academy graduate and combat decorated Navy SEAL with deployments to Iraq, Afghanistan, and Africa. After military service, he attended Harvard Medical School and completed Residency Training in Emergency Medicine at the University of New Mexico. Currently an attending physician at the University of Vermont and Director of Trauma Care in the Department of Emergency Medicine. Founded Veterans for Responsible Leadership and the Vermont Afghan Alliance. Lives in Vermont with his wife Whittney and 4 children.

Zack Czajkowski

Co-Founder & Chief Operating Officer

A political consultant, public affairs professional, and strategist who began his career as a field organizer for Obama's 2012 reelection campaign in Florida. Has managed campaigns from school board to US Senate. After managing Katie Hill's successful red to blue campaign, served as Political Director of The Lincoln Project during the 2020 cycle. Founder and CEO of Brackish Solutions and Director of Public Affairs at Four Corners Strategies. Lives in Durham, NC with his wife Dr. Sarah Cunningham and their rescue bulldog Petunia.

Matthew Hanauer, PhD

Chief Technology Officer

Transforms complex AI concepts into market-ready products that drive measurable business impact. With 15+ years in data science leadership, has delivered over 15 production-grade products generating $15M+ in quantifiable savings. Track record includes revitalizing stalled AI initiatives and bringing them to market within 6-9 months while reducing time-to-market by 4x. Specializes in building healthcare AI solutions and excels at translating stakeholder requirements into scalable AI products with end-to-end expertise from ideation through deployment.

Jordyn Geruso

Chief Marketing Officer

A dedicated professional with a deep passion for public service and community advocacy. After earning her bachelor's degree in Healthcare Management and Marketing, transitioned from Human Resources to the political arena in 2023. Now serves as the Executive Director of Veterans for Responsible Leadership and has contributed to other pro-democracy organizations including Women4US and Valor Media Network. Lives in Montana with her fiancé, their dog, and horse.
