# Civly - AI-Powered Campaign Research and Finance Platform

Automated donor verification, FEC compliance, and campaign intelligence tools for political campaigns

## Platform Overview

Civly is a comprehensive platform that helps political campaigns ensure FEC compliance and conduct opposition research through AI-powered tools. The platform is organized into two main product suites:

### Civly Compliance
End-to-end campaign research and finance compliance management:
- **Donor Verification**: Automated employment, address, and identity verification
- **Compliance Screening**: Check contribution limits, detect prohibited sources, screen watchlists
- **Expense Validation**: AI-powered expense legitimacy analysis for personal use compliance
- **FEC Filing**: Automated generation of FEC-compliant .fec and CSV files
- **Audit Trail**: Complete tracking of all verification decisions and compliance actions

### Civly War Room
Research and intelligence tools for campaigns:
- **Deep Research**: Deep research intelligence platform for opposition research
- **Research War Room**: Content generator with AI chat that creates charts, tables, and campaign materials
- **Research Vault**: Central document storage hub for all research artifacts with tagging and search
- **Background Checks**: Deep donor background checks with AI web search
- **YouTube Analysis**: YouTube and web controversy analysis with transcript deep-dive and chat
- **TikTok Analysis**: TikTok content analysis with video transcripts, controversy detection, and AI chat
- **Political Emails**: Analyze political email campaigns, messaging tactics, and competitive intelligence
- **Congressional Record**: Search congressional floor speeches with two-phase search and filtering
- **FEC Committee Explorer**: Explore FEC committee data with AI-powered chat, charts, and analysis
- **Voice Research**: Real-time voice conversations with AI political consultant about research documents
- **Text to Audio**: Text-to-audio with multiple delivery styles and two-voice podcasts
- **Donor Prospecting**: AI-powered donor prospecting with FEC data and automated outreach
- **Scheduled Reports**: AI podcast script generator with scheduled email delivery
- **Voting Records**: Congressional voting record lookup with AI-powered analysis and chat
